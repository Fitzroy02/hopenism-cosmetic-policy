# SELLER VIDEO COMPLIANCE RULE

## 1. Mandatory Transparency
Sellers must:

- use the marketplace's built-in camera indicator
- never disable or obscure it
- never activate video without explicit user consent

---

## 2. Recording Restrictions
Sellers may not:

- record video without explicit, separate consent
- store recordings outside approved systems
- mislead users about recording status

---

## 3. Enforcement
Violations result in:

- immediate listing suspension
- seller tier downgrade
- possible removal from marketplace
- regulator notification (if required)
