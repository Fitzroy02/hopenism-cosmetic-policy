# INCIDENT RESPONSE PROTOCOL

## Purpose

To manage safety incidents, consumer complaints, and regulatory escalations with clarity and speed.

---

## I. Incident Types

### Type 1 — Minor

- Packaging error
- Incorrect label
- Non-harmful complaint

### Type 2 — Moderate

- Allergic reaction
- Misleading claim
- Missing documentation

### Type 3 — Severe

- Serious undesirable effect (SUE)
- Unsafe ingredient
- Counterfeit product
- Regulatory breach

---

## II. Response Timeline

| Incident Type | Response Time |
|---------------|----------------|
| **Type 1** | 72 hours |
| **Type 2** | 48 hours |
| **Type 3** | 24 hours |

---

## III. Response Steps

1. **Acknowledge** the incident
2. **Suspend** the listing (Type 2–3)
3. **Request** documentation from seller
4. **Run** compliance + risk scan
5. **Interview** seller (if needed)
6. **Determine** corrective action
7. **Notify** Responsible Person
8. **Report** to regulator (Type 3)
9. **Publish** incident summary (transparency)

---

## IV. Corrective Actions

- Listing removal
- Seller suspension
- Tier downgrade
- Mandatory reformulation
- Marketplace-wide warning
- Regulator notification
