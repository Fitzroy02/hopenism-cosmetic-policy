# SELLER AGREEMENT

This agreement defines the obligations of all sellers participating in the Safe Cosmetics Marketplace.

## 1. Eligibility

Sellers must:

- be fully verified
- provide accurate documentation
- comply with UK/EU cosmetic regulations

## 2. Documentation Requirements

Sellers must provide:

- CPSR
- PIF
- label artwork
- ingredient list
- claims justification
- Responsible Person documentation

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

Sellers may not:

- make medical or borderline claims
- list unsafe or untested products
- sell professional-only items to the public
- misrepresent ingredients or concentrations
- import unregulated products

## 4. Accountability

Sellers agree to:

- traceable authorship
- transparent listing history
- immediate suspension for safety violations
- regulatory notification if required

## 5. Enforcement

The marketplace may:

- reject listings
- suspend sellers
- remove products
- escalate to regulators
