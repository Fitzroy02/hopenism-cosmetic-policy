# Seller Tiers

Seller tiers define graduated permissions based on verification depth, compliance history, and operational maturity.

## Tier Model

## Tier 1 — Starter

- Basic identity verification completed.
- Limited listing volume and restricted claim categories.
- Enhanced moderation frequency.

## Tier 2 — Verified

- Business verification and documentation approved.
- Broader listing permissions and standard claim categories.
- Routine compliance reviews.

## Tier 3 — Trusted

- Proven compliance history and high documentation quality.
- Access to advanced listing features and reduced review latency.
- Subject to periodic audits and rapid remediation obligations.

## Escalation and Downgrade

- Sellers may move up after meeting documented criteria.
- Material violations trigger temporary restrictions or tier downgrade.
- Repeat severe violations trigger suspension or removal.
