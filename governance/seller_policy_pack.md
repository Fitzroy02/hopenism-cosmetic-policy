# SELLER POLICY PACK

This pack defines the rules, expectations, and obligations for all marketplace sellers.

---

## I. Eligibility Requirements

Sellers must:

- be fully verified
- provide accurate documentation
- comply with UK/EU cosmetic regulations
- maintain transparency in all listings

---

## II. Documentation Requirements

Mandatory for every product:

- CPSR
- PIF
- Label artwork
- Ingredient list
- Claims justification
- Responsible Person documentation

---

## III. Prohibited Conduct

Sellers may not:

- make medical or borderline claims
- list unsafe or untested products
- sell professional-only items to the public
- misrepresent ingredients or concentrations
- import unregulated products
- attempt to bypass automated checks

---

## IV. Listing Requirements

Every listing must include:

- full INCI
- concentration disclosures (where relevant)
- warnings
- usage instructions
- RP details
- batch number
- PAO symbol

---

## V. Enforcement Actions

The marketplace may:

- reject listings
- suspend sellers
- remove products
- escalate to regulators
- downgrade seller tier

---

## VI. Seller Responsibilities

Sellers must:

- maintain up-to-date documentation
- respond to compliance queries
- report adverse events
- update listings when formulations change

---

## VII. Cultural Commitments

Sellers must align with:

- soft guardrails
- traceable authorship
- protection before profit
- no exploitation of regulatory grey zones
- no enabling unqualified actors
