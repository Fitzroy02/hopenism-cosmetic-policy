# Marketplace Constitution

## Introduction
The Marketplace Constitution outlines the principles and guidelines governing the operations and conduct within the marketplace.

## Guidelines
1. **Transparency**: All transactions must be documented and accessible for review.
2. **Fairness**: All users must have equal opportunity to participate in the marketplace.
3. **Accountability**: Vendors and buyers are accountable for their actions and should abide by the terms set forth in this Constitution.

## Amendments
This Constitution may be amended by a majority vote from the governing body of the marketplace.
