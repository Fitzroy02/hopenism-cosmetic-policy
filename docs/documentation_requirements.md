# Documentation Requirements

This standard defines what evidence and records are required for policy and listing decisions.

## Policy Documents Must Include

- Purpose and scope,
- Definitions of key terms,
- Applicable controls and enforcement pathways,
- Ownership and review cadence,
- Effective date and change history.

## Listing-Related Documents Must Include

- Product identity and ingredient information,
- Claims evidence references,
- Safety warnings and contraindications,
- Region-specific compliance notes where applicable.

## Governance Records Must Include

- Decision rationale,
- Reviewer identity or control source,
- Risk notes and mitigation actions,
- Final status and timestamp.
