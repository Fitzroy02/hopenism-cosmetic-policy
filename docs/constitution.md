# Constitution

The Constitution defines the highest-level commitments of this policy system.

## Purpose

Create cosmetic-related policies that are safe, transparent, and respectful of users.

## Core Principles

1. **Safety First**: User safety takes precedence over speed, novelty, or marketing pressure.
2. **Truthfulness**: Claims must be evidence-based and clearly communicated.
3. **Respect**: Language and policy outcomes must respect dignity, identity, and accessibility.
4. **Accountability**: Decisions are documented, attributable, and reviewable.
5. **Continuous Improvement**: Policies are revised when new evidence or risks emerge.

## Scope

This Constitution applies to:

- Product claims and public messaging.
- Ingredient and formulation policy decisions.
- Internal review and external communication standards.

## Amendment Rule

Constitutional changes require:

- Written proposal,
- Governance review,
- Explicit approval by designated policy owners,
- Changelog entry with rationale and effective date.
