# Regulatory Map (UK/EU)

This high-level map defines the baseline regulatory frameworks and required compliance domains.

## Core Frameworks

### UK

- UK Cosmetics Regulation (retained EU 1223/2009).
- OPSS as key authority.
- UK Responsible Person requirement.

### EU

- Regulation (EC) No 1223/2009.
- CPNP notification.
- EU Responsible Person requirement.

## Mapped Domains

- **Product safety:** CPSR, PIF, GMP.
- **Ingredients:** Annex II (banned), Annex III (restricted), UV filters, preservatives, colourants.
- **Labelling:** INCI, RP details, batch, PAO, warnings, net content, function.
- **Claims:** Common Criteria (legal, truthful, honest, fair, evidence-based).
- **Reporting:** serious undesirable effects to competent authorities.

## Marketplace Position

- Align with both UK and EU baselines.
- Refuse products that cannot clearly sit inside these frameworks.
