# Seller Verification Flow

This flow ensures only legitimate, accountable sellers access marketplace privileges.

## Verification Stages

1. Account creation and policy acknowledgment.
2. Identity and business verification.
3. Documentation review (product and compliance artifacts).
4. Risk screening and initial tier assignment.
5. Activation with monitoring obligations.

## Required Evidence

- Legal entity details where applicable,
- Contact and responsible party information,
- Product documentation required by policy,
- Declarations of claim accuracy and compliance.

## Outcomes

- **Approved**: seller activated at assigned tier.
- **Pending**: additional information required.
- **Rejected**: unresolved integrity or compliance issues.
