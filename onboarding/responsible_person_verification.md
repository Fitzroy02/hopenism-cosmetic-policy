# RESPONSIBLE PERSON VERIFICATION PROCESS

## Purpose

To ensure every product has a legally compliant Responsible Person (RP) under UK/EU law.

---

## I. Required RP Documentation

- RP name and address
- Proof of appointment
- CPSR signed by RP
- PIF maintained by RP
- Safety assessor qualifications
- GMP evidence

---

## II. Verification Steps

1. **Identity Check**
   - Government ID
   - Business registration

2. **Authority Check**
   - Letter of appointment
   - Manufacturer confirmation

3. **Competency Check**
   - Safety assessor qualifications
   - Regulatory training

4. **Documentation Check**
   - CPSR completeness
   - PIF completeness
   - Label compliance

5. **Traceability Check**
   - Batch records
   - Supplier documentation

---

## III. RP Status Levels

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Verified** | Fully compliant |
| **Provisionally Verified** | Minor documentation pending |
| **Unverified** | Cannot act as RP |
