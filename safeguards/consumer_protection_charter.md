# Consumer Protection Charter

Consumers are entitled to safety, honesty, and effective recourse.

## Consumer Rights

1. Right to accurate product and claim information.
2. Right to clear warning labels and usage guidance.
3. Right to report harmful or deceptive listings.
4. Right to timely handling of complaints.
5. Right to non-discriminatory treatment.

## Institutional Duties

- Maintain transparent listing and moderation standards.
- Remove harmful or materially deceptive content quickly.
- Communicate corrective actions when risk is identified.
- Track complaint patterns and improve controls.
