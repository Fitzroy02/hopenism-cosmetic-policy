# PROHIBITED INGREDIENTS

These substances may not appear in any product listed on the marketplace.

## A. Banned Under UK/EU Law

- Hydroquinone (non-prescription)
- Mercury and mercury compounds
- Lead and lead compounds
- Chloroform
- Formaldehyde (above permitted limits)
- Steroids
- Prescription-only actives (e.g., tretinoin)

## B. High-Risk Substances

- Injectable fillers
- Microneedling serums
- Unregulated SPF filters
- Hormonal actives

## C. Unverifiable Imports

Any ingredient without:

- INCI name
- safety data
- traceable origin
