# Listing Rules

These rules apply to all cosmetic listings before publication.

## Mandatory Requirements

- Product identity, ingredients, and intended use are clearly stated.
- Claims are specific, non-deceptive, and evidence-backed.
- Safety warnings, contraindications, and usage limits are visible.
- Prohibited or restricted substances are not presented as compliant.

## Prohibited Practices

- Absolute medical-style claims without approved basis.
- Before/after framing that is manipulative or fabricated.
- Omission of known material risks.
- Language that shames appearance or exploits insecurity.

## Enforcement

- Non-compliant listings are blocked, removed, or returned for correction.
- Repeated violations trigger tier restrictions and account actions.
