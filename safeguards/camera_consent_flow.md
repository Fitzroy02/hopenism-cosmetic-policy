# CAMERA & MICROPHONE CONSENT FLOW

## Step 1 — Pre-Activation Prompt
User sees:

> "This feature requires camera access. You will be visible to the other party."

Buttons:
- **Allow Camera**
- **Cancel**

---

## Step 2 — Microphone Prompt
> "This feature requires microphone access."

Buttons:
- **Allow Microphone**
- **Cancel**

---

## Step 3 — Transparency Confirmation
After activation:

- indicator appears
- user receives a one-time tooltip:
  > "This icon means your camera is active."

---

## Step 4 — Easy Deactivation
User can turn off camera/mic at any time via:

- on-screen toggle
- system controls

---

## Step 5 — Consent Logging
Every activation is logged.
