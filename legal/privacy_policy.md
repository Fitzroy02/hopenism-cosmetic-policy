## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how the Safe Cosmetics Marketplace ("we", "our", "the Marketplace") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data.  
We operate under a safety-first, transparency-first governance model.

---

## 2. Data We Collect
We collect the following categories of data:

### A. Account Information
- Name
- Email address
- Business registration details
- Responsible Person details

### B. Product & Compliance Data
- CPSR
- PIF
- Label artwork
- Ingredient lists
- Claims justification

### C. Camera & Microphone Transparency Data
- Consent status
- Activation timestamps
- Session metadata
- Audit logs

### D. Usage Data
- Login history
- Listing activity
- Moderation interactions

We do **not** collect biometric data, facial recognition data, or unnecessary personal identifiers.

---

## 3. How We Use Data
We use data to:

- verify sellers
- ensure product safety
- maintain compliance
- operate the marketplace
- protect consumers
- investigate incidents
- meet legal obligations

We do **not** sell personal data.

---

## 4. Legal Basis
We process data under:

- **Legitimate interest** (safety, compliance, fraud prevention)
- **Contractual necessity** (seller participation)
- **Legal obligation** (cosmetic regulations, incident reporting)
- **Explicit consent** (camera/microphone activation)

---

## 5. Data Sharing
We may share data with:

- regulatory authorities (when required)
- compliance partners
- technical service providers

We do **not** share data with advertisers or data brokers.

---

## 6. Data Retention
- Compliance documents: retained as required by law
- Audit logs: retained for safety and regulatory purposes
- Camera/microphone logs: retained only as long as necessary

---

## 7. User Rights
Users may:

- access their data
- correct inaccuracies
- request deletion (where legally permitted)
- withdraw consent for camera/microphone use

---

## 8. Security
We use:

- encryption
- access controls
- audit trails
- secure storage
