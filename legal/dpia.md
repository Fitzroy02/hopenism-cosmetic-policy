# DATA PROTECTION IMPACT ASSESSMENT (DPIA)

## 1. Overview
This DPIA evaluates privacy risks associated with operating the Safe Cosmetics Marketplace, including seller verification, product compliance, and camera/microphone transparency.

---

## 2. Processing Activities Assessed
- Seller onboarding
- Documentation verification
- Product listing
- Compliance scanning
- Camera/microphone activation
- Audit logging
- Incident reporting

---

## 3. Risks Identified

### A. Privacy Risks
- Unauthorised access to compliance documents
- Misuse of camera/microphone features
- Over-collection of personal data
- Insufficient user awareness of recording

### B. Safety Risks
- Sellers listing unsafe products
- Incomplete documentation
- Misleading claims

---

## 4. Mitigations

### A. Technical Controls
- Encryption
- Access control
- Immutable audit logs
- Automated compliance checks
- Mandatory camera indicators

### B. Organisational Controls
- Seller qualification tiers
- Moderation workflow
- Incident response protocol
- Responsible Person verification

### C. User Controls
- Explicit consent for camera/microphone
- Clear privacy notices
- Ability to disable camera/microphone

---

## 5. Residual Risk
Low, due to strong governance and transparency requirements.

---

## 6. Review Cycle
Reviewed annually or after major feature changes.
